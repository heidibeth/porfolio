About

I'm originally from New England and I just moved to Indiana last year. I have always had an interest in technology and began teaching myself to code last summer. I enrolled in a coding bootcamp at Eleven Fifty Academy towards the end of last year and I've loved every minute of it.

I enjoy front end work and being able to use my creativity throughout the coding process. I am looking forward to a long career in Web Development and can't wait for it to start.

I have a Bachelor's Degree in Psychology from the University of New Hampshire and I have worked in the healthcare field for the past 7 years. I love learning new things and solving problems. I have a passion for helping people in any way that I can.

I've been vegan for 5 years and I recently started volunteering at a local animal shelter. In my spare time, I enjoy rock climbing, yoga, hiking and starting random art projects that I will never finish.
